Dylan Trapp's Favorite Links

Here is a collection of my favorite Web sites, organized by general catergories. Check them out and feel free to contact me with suggestions or problems. Here are the categories.

  • Gold and Silver Bullion
  • Car Parts
  • Pets and Fish

Gold and Silver Bullion

JMBullion
This is the website I bought my first silver coins from. It has many differet options and I like that there is free shipping on orders over $100. This website sells silver and gold coins, bars, and vintage currency.
APMEX
This is another website very similar to JMBullion. I usually use this website as well to compare pricing between the two to make sure I'm getting the most for my money.
GoldSilver
This is a website to check the "spot" price of gold and silver. The price of gold and silver changes daily so it is important to keep track of the prices.

Car Parts

FPC Euro
This website offers lifetime guarantees on all car parts, period. I can buy brakes, use them for 3 years, and send them back and they will refund you. You can even send back your used oil!
Integrated Engineering
This is a tuning company to retune the ECU (cars computer) in your car. They have retuned my ECU to make my car higher performance.
Rock Auto
This is typically going to be the best prices for all things auto repair. It is hard to find any website that is cheaper but I do not use it very much just because they do not offer lifetime warranty like FPC Euro does.

Pets and Fish

Petco
Petco is a pet store that sells animals, and animal supplies. The reason I like Petco is because they are everywhere and it is really convienant when I need to buy my dog food or get supplies for my aquariums.
Aquarium Co-op
Aquarium Co-op is a website that sells fish, aquarium supplies, and aquarium plants. The reason I like this company is because the owners post a lot of videos on youtube detailing problems and solutions about aquariums, and these videos have proved very useful in my aquarium keeping.
BHB Reptiles
BHB reptiles sells just that, reptiles, from pythons to boas to lizards to scorpians. BHB reptiles sells all types of reptiles. The reason I like BHB reptiles is for the same reason I like Aquarium Co-op, the owners post many videos online about caring for the different types of reptiles, and while I do not have any reptiles of my own, I find the videos entertaining.
